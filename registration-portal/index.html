The next generation of open models is here. Welcome to Build with Gemma - AIMS DTU, a fast-paced, 1-day in-person hackathon where you'll get hands-on with the most capable open models from Google DeepMind.

With the release of Gemma 4, a new family of open models is officially in your hands. Leverage local frontier intelligence, native function calling, and multimodal understanding to tackle core developer challenges.

Tell a story. Show us the problem and how your Gemma 4 application solves it in a powerful way.

Hackathon Tracks

Track 1: AI for Legal Assistance

$1000

Use Gemma to build a multilingual AI legal assistant that a regular citizen can talk to in their own language, describe their situation in plain terms, and walk away with a clear understanding of their rights, their options, and their next steps.

Track 2: AI for Public Safety

$1000

Traditional public safety monitoring suffers from "alarm fatigue" due to passive, high-noise surveillance that lacks semantic context, often failing to distinguish between benign activities and actual emergencies. Current systems face two critical failures: reliance on cloud-based processing creates latency and privacy risks, while "black-box" pattern matching lacks the reasoning layer needed for reliable, forensic-grade verification.
